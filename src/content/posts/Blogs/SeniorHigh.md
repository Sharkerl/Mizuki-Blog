## 方程组

1. **对称式:<br>**
   如: $x ^ {2} + x y + y ^ {2} = 3$ <br>
   <br>
   -> $\left\{ \begin{array}{l} x + y = ?, \\ x y = ?. \end{array} \right.$
$$
\begin{aligned}
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} x ^ {2} + x y + y ^ {2} = 3, \\ 2 x ^ {2} + 3 x y + 2 y ^ {2} = 4. \end{array} \right. \\
\end{aligned}
$$
$\text{ } x+y=a, \quad xy=b 
 x^{2}+xy+y^{2}=a^{2}-b 
 2x^{2}+3xy+2y^{2}=2(x+y)^{2}-xy 
 =2a^{2}-b 
 \therefore \{ a^{2}-b=3 \\\; 2a^{2}-b=4 \}$
$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} - x ^ {2} y ^ {2} = 3, \\ x + y - x y = 4. \end{array} \right. \\
$$
$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} x ^ {2} + 2 x y - 1 0 x = 0, \\ y ^ {2} + 2 x y - 1 0 y = 0. \end{array} \right. \\
$$
$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} \frac {1 0}{x + y} + \frac {3}{x - y} = - 5, \\ \frac {1 5}{x + y} - \frac {2}{x - y} = - 1. \end{array} \right.
$$
